RPG - University student Life

This game is about your life after you enter if IFRN on the Hard level. You have five options to survive this.


Bard character
Warrior character
Rogue character
Mage character
Barbarian character

Bard character

The bard is the one who inspires everyone in an environment, being a great master of the arts. They can also cast spells of enchantment and illusion on their enemies. They are usually chaotic, gallant, brave and mischievous in their essence! Living archives of subjects in general.
Attributes:
Charisma;
Cleverness;
Agility.


Warrior character

They are the ones who are the frontrunners, and who are there to show what they are capable of! Initially inexperienced, they learn through practical activities.
Attributes:
Extra actions;
Strength;
Agility.


Rogue character

They are stealthy, sneaky, deceitful, cunning and good with traps and mechanisms. They are pragmatic and use tactics considered “unfair”, both in combat and outside of it. They are good at dodging and deceiving.
Attributes:
Dexterity;
Intelligence;
Camouflage.


Mage character

They spend sleepless nights studying every gesture and every component of all subjects. Normally, the wizard has a grimoire, a book where he writes down spells and their requirements. This is where he reads, memorizes, and prepares the spells he will use during the day.
Attributes:
Intelligence;
Resistance;
Organization.


Barbarian character

The Barbarian is a being who rejects the rules and customs of society, adopting a life free from laws and order. They are usually always aware of their surroundings on a battlefield, and use hand-to-hand combat.
Attributes:
Fury;
Instinct;
Strength.
